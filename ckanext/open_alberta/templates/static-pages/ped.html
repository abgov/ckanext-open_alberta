Provincial Electoral Division (PED) Profiles

Electoral Boundaries are defined by the Alberta Electoral Divisions Act. Provincial Electoral Divisions (PEDs) are territorial units represented by an elected member to serve in the Legislative Assembly of Alberta.

These profiles provide detailed demographic and socio-economic information for Alberta's Provincial Electoral Divisions, tabulated from Censuses of Canada and National Household Surveys.
