Risco (app contest winner)

2015 Apps for Alberta contest winner - People’s Choice Award (voted by the public): "Risco,” designed by Team R-Squared (University of Calgary students Gabriela Jurca, Megha Chopra, Govind Parmar, and Omar Addam).

This mobile app helps outdoor enthusiasts access maps information on outdoor hazards such as known grizzly bear zones, snake zones, and active wildfires. The app integrates government data and user-submitted information.
