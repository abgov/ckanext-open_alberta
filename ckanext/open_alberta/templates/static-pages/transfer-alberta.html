Transfer Alberta

Knowing ahead of time what courses and programs transfer between colleges and universities can help you better plan your future, save your time and money. The Transfer Alberta App provides students and secondary/post-secondary professionals with an informative and easy to use search tool to support post-secondary planning, admissions, and transfers. This tool provides access to Alberta post-secondary transfer agreements, as well as high school upgrading and provincial course equivalency information.

The Transfer Alberta App provides:

  • Course/program list searches for high school upgrading or post- secondary transfer, by school and year
  • Comparison of course/program transfer agreements for multiple institutions
  • The ability to save your search results and /or share your results via email
  • Who to call for admissions help at each post-secondary institution.

Provided through the leadership of the Alberta Council on Admission and Transfer (ACAT) and sponsored by Innovation and Advanced Education, this free Transfer Alberta App is here to support your post-secondary pathway(s).

For more information about the App, please visit TransferAlberta.ca or contact ACAT@gov.ab.ca
