Live Alberta (app contest winner)

2015 Apps for Alberta contest winner - Best Student App (created by a student): “Live Alberta,” designed by MacEwan University student Karo Castro- Wunsch.

This web app combines text and visuals to convey a wealth of easy-to- arrange information on cities across Alberta. The app is for users looking for the Alberta city that best meets their needs. This could be a travel destination, a place to live, or a locale to start or relocate a business.

This app was created by a private citizen or organization.
