511 Alberta

511 Alberta is the source for Alberta’s official road reports. The 511 Alberta app provides information on Alberta’s Highway Network, including:

  • Road Conditions
  • Road Incidents
  • Citizen Reporting
  • Snowplows Tracker
  • Roadside Camera Images
  • Construction / Maintenance Activities
  • Traffic Status
  • Ferry Crossings
  • Border Crossings
  • Weather
