GeoDiscover Alberta

The GeoDiscover Alberta Program fosters collaboration and innovation in information sharing. GeoDiscover Alberta strives to be the main access point for easily integrated, authoritative and usable geospatial data and services.

GeoDiscover Alberta will change the way people interact with the earth and each other by creating a trusted, easy-to-use environment for geospatial data and information sharing.
